# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We want participation in Curated Codex Skills to be safe, respectful, and
useful regardless of identity, background, experience, or ability. Be kind,
assume good intent while addressing impact, give actionable feedback, and
accept responsibility when a contribution causes harm.

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized conduct, deliberate disruption,
personal attacks, and publishing another person's private information are not
acceptable in project spaces.

## Scope and enforcement

This policy applies to repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews,
and other spaces where someone represents the project. Maintainers may edit or
remove content, restrict participation, or ban a participant when necessary to
protect the community. Decisions should be proportionate, documented when safe,
and respectful of reporter privacy.

Report platform abuse privately through
[GitHub Support](https://support.github.com/contact/report-abuse). For other
conduct concerns, contact the maintainer through the private contact method on
the [maintainer profile](https://github.com/George930502). Do not open a public
issue containing sensitive personal information.
